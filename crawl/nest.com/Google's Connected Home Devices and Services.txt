Google's connected home devices and servicesLast updated: October 6, 2020<p>Google’s connected home devices and services consist of the devices and services listed below that use Google Accounts and carry the Google Nest, Google Home, Nest, Google Wifi or Chromecast brand.<br>
<br>
<strong>Note</strong>: Google’s commitment to privacy in the home — outlined in this guide — applies to Google’s connected home devices and services listed below, but only when they are used with Google Accounts.
This means that Google’s commitment to privacy in the home will also apply to people who migrate from Nest Accounts to Google Accounts. Learn about migrating from a Nest Account to a Google Account.
</p>
